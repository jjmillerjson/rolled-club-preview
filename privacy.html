Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026 · Version 1.0

1. Who is responsible for your data

ROLLED LIMITED (company number 15609887, registered at 7 St. Petersgate, Stockport, England, SK1 1EB) is the data controller for personal data collected through the Rolled Club Card. We are registered with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) under registration reference ZB945189. Contact us about your data at privacy@eatrolled.com.

2. What we collect

  • Account details: first name, email address, password (stored securely hashed — never in plain text), date of birth, phone number, gender (optional).
  • Marketing preferences: your email and SMS marketing opt-in choices and when you set them.
  • Loyalty activity: stamps earned, cards completed, rewards earned and redeemed, membership tier, special cards held, the location and time of your visits, and which staff member referred you or which sign-up source / QR code you joined through (we use this to credit staff sign-ups and to understand where members come from).
  • Technical data: limited device/browser information and whether you've added a wallet pass, used to run the app and your Apple/Google Wallet pass. If you install our app and turn on notifications, we use them to send reward and offer alerts — you can turn these off anytime in your device settings.

What we don't collect: we do not process or store your payment card details — payment is taken separately at the till (for example via the in-store card terminal / point-of-sale), and those details never reach the Club Card. We also don't track your precise device location; for loyalty activity we only record which participating store you visited.

3. Why we use it, and our lawful basis

  • To run the loyalty programme (create your account, track stamps/rewards, show your card and wallet pass) — performance of a contract.
  • To send service messages (email verification, password reset, account notices) — performance of a contract.
  • To send marketing (offers, birthday rewards, reminders) by email and/or SMS — your consent, which you can withdraw at any time.
  • To prevent fraud and abuse, keep the programme secure, and understand and improve it — our legitimate interests.

4. Personalisation & profiling

To make offers relevant we analyse your loyalty activity to group customers (for example by how recently and often you visit) and to tailor which messages or rewards you're shown. This is profiling for marketing relevance only — we do not make solely-automated decisions that have a legal or similarly significant effect on you, and you can object to this kind of processing at any time by contacting us or opting out of marketing.

5. Who we share it with (sub-processors)

We share data only with service providers who help us run the programme, under contract and only as needed. We do not sell your personal data.

  • Resend — service email (verification, password reset, notices).
  • Me&u Connect / Sproutsend — marketing campaign management and SMS/email sends.
  • Apple (PassKit) — Apple Wallet pass storage and updates.
  • Google (Google Wallet API) — Google Wallet pass storage and updates.
  • Netlify — hosting of the web app.
  • Railway — backend hosting and our database (UK/EU region).

6. International transfers

Where a provider processes data outside the UK/EU, we rely on appropriate safeguards (such as UK adequacy regulations or Standard Contractual Clauses) to protect it.

7. Cookies & local storage

The app uses only strictly necessary functional storage on your device (a service worker and local/session storage) to keep you signed in and let the app work offline. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking, so no cookie consent banner is required.

8. How we keep your data secure

We protect your data with measures appropriate to its sensitivity: encryption in transit (HTTPS), passwords stored only as secure one-way hashes, access restricted to authorised staff and processors, and hosting with reputable providers. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable steps to guard against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse, and will notify you and the ICO of a personal data breach where the law requires.

9. How long we keep it

  • We keep your account data for as long as your account is active.
  • Accounts inactive for 3 years are automatically deleted, along with their personal data, except where we must keep limited records longer for legal or accounting reasons.
  • If you close your account, we delete or anonymise your personal data promptly, subject to the same legal-retention exceptions.

10. Your rights

Under UK GDPR / the Data Protection Act 2018 you have the right to: access your data; correct it; delete it; restrict or object to processing (including profiling for marketing); data portability; and withdraw marketing consent at any time (via the unsubscribe link in any marketing email/SMS, in your app profile, or by contacting us). To exercise any right, email privacy@eatrolled.com — we'll respond within one month. You also have the right to complain to the ICO (ico.org.uk).

11. Children

The Club Card is not directed at children. You must be at least 16 to join.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date shows the current version; material changes will be notified via the app or email.
